The philosophy

You do not need to become
someone new. You need to come home to who you already are.

Lumière began on a rain-soft morning in the hills above Ubud, when our founder realised that every pose she had ever chased was really a way of asking to be held. So we built something quieter. No performance. No perfect body. Just breath, light, and the radical practice of treating yourself with tenderness.

Each retreat is a cinematic pause in an over-lit life — a place to move slowly, feel fully, and fall back in love with being alive in your own skin.

Your guide

Amara Devi

Founder & lead teacher · 500hr E-RYT

Amara spent a decade as a performer before her body finally asked her to stop proving and start listening. She trained in Rishikesh, studied somatics in Berlin, and now teaches a practice rooted in one belief: that self-love is not a feeling you wait for, but a way of moving you choose.

Her classes are unhurried, deeply embodied, and quietly funny. You will leave not more flexible, necessarily — but more forgiving of yourself.

"I don't teach you to touch your toes. I teach you to touch your own life more gently."
