Manifesto

We reject the idea that self-love is a bubble bath. It is the daily, unglamorous work of choosing yourself on purpose.

Yoga at Lumière is not about touching your toes or performing serenity for a camera. It is a rehearsal for how you treat yourself off the mat — how you speak in your own head, how you rest without guilt, how you hold a boundary without apology.

We keep the room warm, the lighting honest, and the ego at the door. No mirrors. No competition. No spiritual bypassing. Just a strong body, a settled nervous system, and a teacher who actually sees you.

The Teacher

“I built the studio I needed at my lowest — one that asked nothing of me but showing up.”

Eléna Voss (E-RYT 500) has taught for over a decade across Lisbon, Bali, and Berlin. After burning out in a career that ran on her exhaustion, she rebuilt her life on the mat — and rewrote how she taught. Her method fuses strong vinyasa, somatic release, and trauma-informed care into a practice that's rigorous but never punishing.

She is certified in Yoga Nidra and restorative therapeutics, and mentors the next generation of teachers through Lumière's 200-hour training.
