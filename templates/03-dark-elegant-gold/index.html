The philosophy

We don't teach you to fix yourself.
We help you meet yourself.

So much of modern wellness is quietly cruel — one more thing to optimise, one more version of you to chase. Lumière began as a refusal of all that. A room lit only by candles. A practice that asks nothing of you but your presence.

Here, the mat is not a place to become someone new. It is where you remember who you have always been. We move slowly. We breathe deeply. We let the nervous system soften until self-love stops being an idea and becomes a felt sense in the body.

  • Nervous-system first

    Trauma-aware, unhurried sequencing that signals safety to the body.

  • Ritual, not routine

    Candlelight, scent and sound turn each class into a devotional act.

  • Radical gentleness

    No pushing, no performance. Progress measured in kindness, not shapes.

Your guide

Isabelle Moreau

Founder & lead teacher

Isabelle spent a decade in the fashion industry learning, in her words, “every possible way to abandon myself.” Yoga found her at her most depleted — and slowly, candle by candle, taught her to return.

Now an E-RYT 500 teacher trained in trauma-informed and somatic practice, she has guided more than seven thousand women through the quiet work of self-love. Her teaching is unhurried, deeply human, and unmistakably warm.

“You do not have to earn your own tenderness. You were always allowed to be gentle with yourself.”
