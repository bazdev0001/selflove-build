01 — Philosophy

We believe self-love is not a mood. It is a practice you return to, breath by breath.

Lumière began in a borrowed studio in Copenhagen with six women, a stack of wool blankets, and a single intention: to make room for the parts of ourselves we usually rush past. Ten years on, that intention is unchanged.

Our teaching draws on restorative and yin traditions, somatic awareness, and the unfashionable idea that you are already enough. We do not chase the next shape. We stay long enough to feel what is already here.

03 — Your teacher

Elise Marchand

Founder · E-RYT 500 · Somatic practitioner

Elise trained in restorative and yin yoga across India and southern France before founding Lumière in 2016. Her teaching is quiet, precise, and deeply human — shaped as much by years of her own recovery from burnout as by any lineage.

“I am not interested in fixing anyone,” she says. “Nothing about you is broken. My work is simply to hold the room steady enough that you can put yourself down for an hour and feel what remains.”

— Elise
