Why we move

Yoga that likes you back.

Lumière isn't about touching your toes or contorting into the perfect pose for the perfect photo. It's a practice of returning — to a body you're allowed to enjoy, a breath that's yours to slow down, a self worth celebrating on an ordinary Tuesday.

Every class is built around one radical idea: you are not a project to fix, you are a person to befriend. We bring the color, the warmth and the good playlists. You bring you.

Meet your teacher

Hi, I'm Naïma.

Ten years ago I walked into a studio hating my reflection. Yoga didn't hand me a new body — it handed me back the one I already had, and taught me to love it out loud.

I trained across Lagos, Lisbon and London (RYT-500), and I built Lumière for the person I used to be: the one at the back of the room, convinced she didn't belong. You belong. Let's make that undeniable.
