01 — Philosophy

We do not add.
We uncover.

So much of modern wellness is a striving — one more pose, one more goal, one more version of yourself to become. Here we practice the opposite. We remove. We soften. We let the noise settle like silt in still water until what remains is simply you, already whole.

Every class is built around ma — negative space. The rest between breaths. The hush after a bell. The room left empty so that presence has somewhere to arrive. Self-love, we've found, is not something you achieve. It is what is left when you stop leaving yourself.

— founded in Kyoto & Sussex, 2016

Three ways to begin

Small groups, long silences, no mirrors. Choose the doorway that feels like rest, not effort.

03 — Teacher

Mira Aoyama

Founder & guiding teacher

Mira trained for a decade between a Kyoto zendo and the yoga rooms of London before realising the two were teaching her the same quiet thing. Her work sits at the meeting point of Zen and asana — spare, precise, and unhurried.

She teaches almost nothing, and it changes everything. There are no corrections here, no achievement, no perfect shape to reach for. Only the invitation, again and again, to soften and to stay.

“You are not a problem to be solved. You are a garden to be tended — slowly, and in season.”
