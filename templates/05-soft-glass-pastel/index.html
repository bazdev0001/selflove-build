The philosophy

Yoga as an act of gentleness, not achievement.

We built Lumière for the perfectionists, the over-givers and the quietly exhausted. Here, the mat is not another place to strive — it is a place to arrive. Every sequence is an invitation to meet yourself with the same warmth you offer everyone else.

  • ✿

    Nervous-system first

    Slow, breath-led movement that signals safety to your body.

  • ☾

    Body-neutral cueing

    Language that honours every shape, every day, every mood.

  • ✷

    A real community

    Small circles where you are known by name, not a number.

Your teacher

Elodie Rousseau

Founder · 500hr RYT · Trauma-informed facilitator

After a decade of chasing the "perfect" practice, Elodie burned out — and found her way back through the slow, unglamorous work of self-kindness. Lumière is everything she wished someone had offered her then: a place to be soft on purpose.

She has guided over twelve thousand students across forty countries, trained at the Himalayan Institute, and believes that rest is a radical, beautiful act.
