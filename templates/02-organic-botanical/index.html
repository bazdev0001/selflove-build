The philosophy

Self-love isn't a reward you earn. It's the ground you stand on.

We built Lumière on a quiet idea: that you don't need to be fixed. Our practice isn't about burning calories or nailing a shape — it's about listening inward until the noise settles and something kinder answers back.

Every session is rooted in the seasons. We move with the light, brew our own herbal blends, and keep classes small enough that you're a person, never a number on a mat.

  • Rooted in nature

    Botanical rituals, plant medicine and natural light shape every practice.

  • Softness over striving

    No mirrors, no ranking. Just breath, permission, and rest.

  • Held in community

    Small circles of eight, so you're always seen and supported.

Your guide

Elowen Hart

Founder & lead teacher · 500hr RYT

Twelve years ago I burned out completely — a knot of ambition with nothing left in the tank. Yoga didn't make me more productive. It made me kinder to the person doing the work. Lumière is the studio I wish I'd found back then.

I trained in restorative and yin traditions across Bali and Devon, studied herbalism, and now teach the practice that put me back together: slow, unhurried, and rooted in the belief that you are already enough.

“ You don't have to earn your rest. Come as you are — we'll take it from there.

— Elowen
